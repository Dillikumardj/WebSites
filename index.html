New modes of the season

The Domination Mode

This mode will be played in a new map called "Town".There will be two teams -- Red and Blue with four players in each team.The map consist of three bases.For a particular duration one base is activated.Both teams must try to capture and hold the base to win.Unlimited respawn can be made if they get killed.There will be one round in each base, the team capturing two bases will be the winner.

The Payload Mode

Pubg for a first time introduced an aerial combat.The map will be same as erangal but it contains some cool features like 'Recall','new guns' exclusive to the mode,helicopter,etc. The map will be added in the arcade section.'Recall' feature enable players to respawn if their teammates are eliminated from the match with one condition that their id should be recovered from them before 180 seconds.Each player will be provided with an id during the start of the match. The new guns are rocket launchers,grenade launchers and mini - guns.

The RageGare Mode

Get into the action directly is the main idea of the mode.This mode will be added in the arcade section.The players will be provided with a car.Players can chose their cars before the start of the match.It is team vs team match with each team consisting of 6 player.One car will carry two player among them one has to drive and shoot with the weapon fitted in the car but he can shoot only the car infront of it and another one has to shoot all the cars around them.RPG-7,MGL,M249 are the guns available in this mode.

New Character

SARA
The new character is a female called as sara.She is a proficient driver.Like victor, this character is also upgradable. She will have exceptional driving skills and she will offer increased resistance to vehicle damage.She excels at reinforcing the vehicles so they take less damage.All this abilities are restricted to EvoGround modes.To get the sara character players must complete some acheivements. with the completion of each acheivements, some uc vouchers will be provided.If you collect 600uc you can get the sara character.When you play using sara character you will be provided with some points.With that points you can open a crate that is filled with the costumes, skins and emotes of sara.The items you get from the crate is exclusive to sara so you cannot use it for other characters.Sara also has a winning emote which is awesome. New Events

Sankrati Event

Sankrati Event
Event Rules: 1) Complete the daily missions to receive free dice. 2) Roll the dice to proceed to the next levels. 3) Daily mission will fresh every 24 hours. 4) Land on level 120 exactly to get the rewards. 5) If the player is at level 119 and the player gets 3 when he rolls the dice, he will be moves to level 118.

Winner Event

Winner Event
Win some real items like a Golden Helmet , AirPods and some gift cards from play store and app stores.During the event period, red packets will be droping in the lobby. Collect them and open to see the rewards you get.Each day players can collect atmost 5 red packets.

Diwali Event
